Hostel Rules & Regulations
To maintain discipline and ensure a conducive environment for learning

1
It is compulsory for every boarder that he should take the meal in hostel otherwise his seat will be cancelled. Special permission will have to be taken from warden in case of having a meal from outside permanently.

2
Allotment of seats to students will be according to the conveniences & management of the hostel committee.

3
All the students should be present in their respective rooms at 7:00 pm in winter and 9:00 pm in summer. Study timing is reserved from 9:00 pm to 11:00 pm in summer and 7:30 pm to 10:30 pm in winter.

4
Students who want to go on long leave are advised to submit their luggage to the hostel warden and pay the dues.

5
Students who often remain absent from classes can be expelled from the hostel.

6
It is a crime to remain absent from the hostel at night without any cogent reason. Rs.500/- at least will be fined if violated.

7
Immoral activities such as gambling, drinking, and other narcotics are strictly prohibited. Guilty students will be expelled immediately.

8
Residents of hostel are bound to abide by rules & regulations, otherwise, expulsion or heavy fine will be imposed.

9
Any student found guilty of misconduct or disobedience will face disciplinary action.

10
The real objective of students is to study. Creating fuss or keeping weapons is strictly against the law and will lead to expulsion.

11
The allotment of hostel seat is for a maximum period of three years as per session of DAE admission. No allotment for short courses.

12
Free mess is not allowed for any student.

13
Every student must pay hostel security and mess advance.

14
Application form submission is required for hostel allotment.

15
Mess bill must be paid by the 10th of each month; Rs.10 per day fine after due date. Defaulters of one month will be expelled.

16
Every boarder is responsible for caring for hostel property. Any damage must be compensated or expulsion will follow.

17
Do not keep valuable things or large sums of money. Hostel is not responsible for any loss.

18
All students must vacate the hostel during vacations.

19
Students cannot live in hostel after their last exam.

20
Students must take morning exercise and participate in evening sports.

21
Hostel warden has full authority to enforce rules and maintain discipline.

22
In case of any ambiguity, the principal's decision will be final.
